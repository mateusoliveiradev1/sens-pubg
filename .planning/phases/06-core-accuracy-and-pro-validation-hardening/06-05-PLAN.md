<threat_model>
Scope: calibration, strict release benchmark, golden scripts, report generation, and commercial readiness.

Threats:
- HIGH: benchmark passes while confidence is overconfident on reviewed clips. Mitigation: calibration report blocks commercial readiness on overconfidence and blocker mistakes.
- HIGH: no permissioned real/pro corpus exists, but report claims commercial readiness. Mitigation: readiness status is partial/blocked when reviewed permissioned clips are absent.
- HIGH: coach or sensitivity unsafe handoffs are hidden inside aggregate pass rates. Mitigation: calibration report has separate sensitivity safety and coach handoff safety rows.
- MEDIUM: strict gate becomes a second untracked command. Mitigation: extend `benchmark:release` as the canonical strict gate and keep docs/scripts aligned.
</threat_model>

- blocker accuracy;
