<threat_model>
Scope: captured/real/pro corpus artifacts, promotion, docs, and internal reviewer workflow.

Threats:
- HIGH: public videos are treated as trainable validation data without permission. Mitigation: consent schema and promotion blockers require permission/trainability for validation use.
- HIGH: withdrawn clips remain in goldens/baselines. Mitigation: withdrawal status blocks promotion and docs require quarantine/rebaseline.
- HIGH: raw clips or derivatives are redistributed beyond consent. Mitigation: consent scopes distinguish private raw, report metadata, thumbnails/frames, and derivative permissions.
- MEDIUM: consent schema becomes vague and cannot be audited later. Mitigation: per-clip fields include contributor reference, date, purpose, authorization, reviewer, status, and withdrawal reason.
</threat_model>
