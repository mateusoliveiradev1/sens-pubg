<threat_model>
Scope: analyze page, upload states, result report, spray visualization, lock/quota states, and related view models.

Threats:
- HIGH: Premium visuals make weak/inconclusive evidence look strong. Mitigation: decision ladder/evidence states drive tones, summary copy, and subdued low-evidence visual treatment.
- HIGH: Free report hides truth evidence behind Pro locks. Mitigation: Free always shows verdict, mastery, confidence, coverage, blockers/inconclusive, and short next step.
- HIGH: Client UI becomes authoritative for quota or Pro. Mitigation: keep `getAnalysisSaveAccess`, `saveAnalysisResult`, and `PremiumProjectionSummary` as server-derived facts.
- MEDIUM: Upload guidance becomes tutorial-heavy or blocks real use. Mitigation: progressive guidance, one primary action, clear invalid/processing/error states.
- MEDIUM: Canvas labels/legend overlap or lack accessible text. Mitigation: stable aspect ratio, textual summary, mobile legend layout, no fake reference trail.
</threat_model>
